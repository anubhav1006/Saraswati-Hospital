Dr. Rajeshwar Shah

History

Dr. Rajeshwar Shah started his career as a senior resident/surgeon in Hospital Holyfamily in 1995.After few years he joined Hospital MGS in punjabi Bagh,West Delhi as a consultant surgeon, he was also a visiting Consultant at Jaipur Golden Hospital. Dr. Shah was trained for Laproscopy surgeries under Dr. Chaube ,a famous Laproscopic Surgeon at Ganga Ram Hospital. After gaining experience on different types of surgeries he decided to run his own hospital in 2004 and has been working independently since then .He is also political figure and an active member in NPP(National People Party) led by late P.A.Sangma .He also has been awarded for serving the poor by DMA.

Education Qualifications

  • MS(Surgery) : Moti Lal Nehru Medical College,Allahabad
  • Training(Laproscopy) : Ganga Ram Hospital
